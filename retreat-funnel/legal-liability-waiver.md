# Liability waiver, insurance clause & consent — DWS retreat (DRAFT)

> ⚠️ **Have a lawyer review before use.** This is a practical, plain-language
> draft for a high-risk activity (Deep Water Solo) delivered in Vietnam by a
> local operator, sold from Québec/Canada. Waiver enforceability varies by
> jurisdiction; a Québec/Canada-licensed lawyer should confirm wording, the
> governing-law clause, and how liability is shared between Core Aliveness
> Project and the local operator. Participants should sign **before the balance
> is due** (and keep a copy). Minors are not accepted (18+ only).

## B. Insurance clause — mandatory (FR + EN)

**FR** — *Assurance obligatoire.* Chaque participant·e doit souscrire, à ses
frais, une **assurance voyage couvrant les sports extrêmes et l'escalade**,
incluant les **frais médicaux d'urgence et le rapatriement**, valide pendant
toute la durée de la retraite au Vietnam. Une **preuve d'assurance** (police +
numéro + coordonnées de l'assureur) doit être transmise à l'Organisateur **au
plus tard 30 jours avant le départ**. Sans preuve valide, la participation peut
être refusée sans remboursement du solde. L'assurance de l'Opérateur local, le
cas échéant, ne remplace pas l'assurance personnelle du participant.

**EN** — *Mandatory insurance.* Each participant must hold, at their own cost,
**travel insurance covering extreme sports and climbing**, including **emergency
medical care and repatriation**, valid for the full duration of the retreat in
Vietnam. **Proof of insurance** (policy, number, insurer contact) must be sent
to the Organizer **at least 30 days before departure**. Without valid proof,
participation may be refused with no refund of the balance. Any local operator
insurance, if applicable, does not replace the participant's personal coverage.
